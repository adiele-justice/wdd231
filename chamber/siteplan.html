Community Garden Initiative

Site Name

The chosen name for this project is "Community Garden Initiative". This name reflects the project's goal of bringing together local residents to cultivate a shared gardening space, promote sustainability, and strengthen community ties.

Optional domain availability: communitygardeninitiative.com

Site Purpose

The Community Garden Initiative website serves as a central platform for local gardening enthusiasts to connect, share knowledge, and stay informed about garden activities and events.

  • The site offers information on membership options and how to get involved in the garden.
  • It provides details about upcoming events such as planting days, workshops, and community potlucks.
  • A blog featuring gardening tips, success stories, and seasonal planting guides will be available.
  • A forum for members to exchange advice and resources will foster collaboration and support.

Scenarios

  • What are the benefits of joining a community garden, and how can I become a member?
  • Where can I find information about upcoming workshops and events in the garden?
